Apocalyptic Survival

This game is a openworld 2D topdown survival based fighting and recource gathering game set in Wisconsin which involves Hunting, Gathering and Crafting. You have one life, defeat the undead or be killed, no quicksaves, no respawns. Survival Of The Fittest.
